What is Task Buddy ?

Are you struggling with procrastination? Do you feel its hard to keep track of your tasks and you aren't getting the most out of your day?

Task Buddy aims to resolve these problems. Our app will be designed to help you stay organized, track your habits, and accomplish your goals, all while nurturing a virtual pet. Your pet will serve as your digital companion, encouraging you to complete your daily habits and to-do lists efficiently by keeping you responsible for the pets health.

More Info

Create and Track the Perfect To Do List

TaskBuddy allows you to create a to do list where you can add, edit or delete a task. Where you will also have the option to mark these tasks, as "in progress" or "completed" to track your progress for the week or the month. Every time your to do list is completed, you will be rewarded by some coins that you can later use for your pet. Through these coins, you can visit the Virtual Pet Shop and buy clothes and food to maintain the well being of your pet.

Why the Virtual Pet?

We added the virtual pet companion to incentivize the user to be more proactive about their own task, habits, and goals. By nurturing your pet, you develop a sense of responsibility and attachment, further fueling your motivation to stay productive and achieve your goals.

This app harnesses the power of technology to enhance our productivity and motivate ourselves through the caring of a digital pet and healthy competition with other users.
